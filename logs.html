Maintenance Logs

These are maintenance logs I've written for the Columbia Makerspace. While they are specific to our lab, some equipment information may be valuable. They are all hosted on Slack's post system, since they were originally written for our Slack channel. Please feel free to contact me with questions or corrections.

April 2018

  • 4/1/18 Laser Cutter Fire
    The Thunderlaser Nova 51 laser cutter was used without its air compressor on (for the air assist), resulting in fire damage. This log documents the steps taken to return the cutter into clean, working condition.

March 2018

  • 3/15/18 Sewing Machine Bobbin Case
    The Singer 5532 sewing machine is stitching inconsistently and chewing up thread. The wheel turns rough manually. The bobbin case isn't rotating correctly and getting caught, so it needs to be replaced.
  • 3/15/18 Laser Cutter PSU Diagnosis
    Our Thunderlaser Nova 51 suddenly stops cutting, even though the head traces the design correctly. I document the process of diagnosing the exact issue, which is a blown laser PSU. A replacement is on its way.
  • 3/14/18 3D Printer Broken Fan
    An incorrectly run fan cable from a previous head rebuild is inevitably caught between joints and cut. I disassemble the head and replace the fan, this time running the cable correctly. Ultimaker 3 Extended.
  • 3/5/18 3D Printer Askew Axis
    Askew sliders cause prints to be slightly skewed, resulting in boxes with non-90deg corners. Repositioning the slider results in a quick fix. Ultimaker 2+ Extended.
